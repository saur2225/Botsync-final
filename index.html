Our automation systems are custom-built for your unique needs, keeping in mind the nuances of your industry.

MAG ensure safety through:

  • SLAM Navigation: SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) helps AMRs map their surroundings and navigate in real time. Using LiDAR and cameras, they adapt to obstacles instantly, rerouting smoothly.
  • Safety and Status Lights: A purple light warns workers of the AMR’s approach, while status lights change as the robot moves through different tasks, keeping operators informed.
  • External Sensors and Cameras: These sensors act as the robot’s eyes, detecting objects and rerouting as needed to avoid collisions.
  • Zone Assignments: Operators can assign and adjust work zones, allowing the AMRs to adapt to changing layouts or new production lines.
  • SyncOS™ Integration: SyncOS™ connects AMRs with other machines (forklifts, conveyors, WMS), ensuring smooth data flow and easy troubleshooting.

SyncOS™ is an interoperable platform that simplifies automation by enabling seamless integration and data transfer between different robotic systems from multiple vendors. It has a no-code interface, making it user-friendly for operators without advanced technical skills, while maximizing productivity and scalability with drag-and-drop workflow management.

Our solutions are designed for seamless integration with your current operations, operating alongside your existing workflows without disrupting them. SyncOS, our vendor-agnostic platform, ensures that any AMR or AGV (not just Botsync’s) can integrate smoothly with existing systems, regardless of the manufacturer. With pre-built integrations for systems like Warehouse Management Systems (WMS) and an easy-to-use interface, managing and monitoring your robotic systems requires minimal technical expertise.
