About Us

Botsync is Asia’s leading provider of robotic automation solutions, transforming enterprise supply chains with cutting-edge technology. Our autonomous robot fleet reduces manual operations, while our no-code software platform empowers operators to integrate automation systems quickly—without the need for specialists or production downtime.

We’ve revolutionised operations for industry leaders like Ford, Coca-Cola, and Kimberly-Clark, allowing them to boost throughput, reduce downtime, and improve efficiency in record time
